Our Story

Born in
Addis.
Built for

Premium+ Production was founded on a simple belief: audiences remember work that feels crafted. The image, edit, sound, color, and story all have to carry the same intention.

We bring a filmmaker's eye to commercials, branded films, documentaries, music videos, and social content, then shape each deliverable for the platform where it will live.

Today, we work with brands across East Africa and beyond, handling the journey from brief and treatment to shoot, edit, color grade, sound, and final campaign delivery.
